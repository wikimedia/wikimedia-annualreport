Knowledge is joy!

People make Wikipedia great.

Whether it’s writing whole articles, correcting tiny typos, making small donations or just using Wikipedia for research, millions of people around the world unite around the goals of the world’s largest online information compendium, making free knowledge available to anyone in the world.

The people behind the Wikimedia projects come from many cultures, have many backgrounds, but share that one, simple mission.

Here are just some of the tens of thousands of editors, across hundreds of languages, who take pride in developing Wikimedia projects and helping guide others in doing the same.
